Welcome! Let me tell you about myself

Hi, I’m Susan. I’m a college graduate and graphic designer living in sunny Sunnyvale.

I am also a grease mom to three beautiful and temperamental machines, you can read more about my 1965 Vespa, a 1971 Triumph Bonneville, and a 1961 Datsun Pick-up truck on my blog here. I’ve grown up in California my whole life and am lucky to pursue my passion in my hometown.

Sketches by Susan, my design firm, is a studio where we bring your vision to life. Whether it be restyling a personal blog, revamping your business brand or a creative arts project we’ve done it all. My studio is rooted in timeless designs with a personal touch that makes them unique to you.

If you aren’t sure where to start check out my portfolio here or read some of my blog. I look forward to working together!
